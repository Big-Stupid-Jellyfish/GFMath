AK-47 with 74M

By BigStupidJellyfish | 2022-May-29

AK-74M is expected to release next month as a new craftable doll, with an interesting skill:

Katyusha ICD 0s/CD 0s

T-Dolls in the echelon carrying an [AK] are enhanced;

  • [AK-74M]: All T-Dolls wielding an AK in the echelon gain 5% damage and rate of fire.
  • [AK-47]: "Strike Focus" damage and accuracy buffs are swapped.
  • [AK-74U]: "Purge Reaction" is buffed to a passive.
  • [AK-12]: "Eye of the White Mastiff" grants an additional 35% critical hit damage while it is active.
  • [AK-15]: Attacks will always trigger her "Anger" effect.
  • [AK-Alfa]: Has perfect accuracy when targeting enemy units in front of her.

She provides unique passive buffs to all the AK-style dolls listed.

There's a whole lot to consider there, so for now let's just look at her interaction with AK-47. After all, AW+ has finally been announced for Q3 per Q&A - maybe AK-47's scope SPEQ and this is enough to be worth considering.

Strike Focus ICD 5s/CD 8s

Increase damage by 35% and accuracy by 100% for 5 seconds.

This skill is the main reason why AK-47 has never been recommended, with poor DPS stats as the nail in the coffin. A 35% multiplier is simply terrible even by 3-star standards, and the accuracy buff mostly just brings her accuracy back to normal levels.

But switching that to a 100% FP multiplier, plus 44% crit damage from her best scope, has a shot at being worth something.

DPS calculations#

AK-47's scope favors crit builds, which are generally better for ARSMGs anyways. Let's look at the in- and out-of-skill DPS of her and a few other options, assuming a Parachute fairy without talent.

Configuration Basic DPS In-skill DPS
AK-47 (VFL+Chip) 1050.1 1656.6
AK-47 (SPEQ+Chip) 1395.9 2202.1
AK-47 (AK-74M, VFL+Chip) 1471.7 3181.4
AK-47 (AK-74M, SPEQ+Chip) 2029.4 4387.0
G41 (VFL+SPEQ) 1476.3 2583.4
G41 (AUG, VFL+SPEQ) 1709.6 2991.9
G41 (M4A1, VFL+SPEQ) 2014.1 3524.6

AK-47's SPEQ alone is clearly not enough to make her useful. However, pairing that with AK-74M appears to bring her DPS to a similar range as G41 (+M4A1 & SPEQ), one of the better "boring" direct-fire DPS ARs.

We have a lead, but we're a bit short of a full argument. Let's go deeper.

Damage over time#

AK-74M's tiles aren't too far off from M4's, so let's suppose our third AR doesn't care either way. First, we'll chart the relative cumulative damage over time from G41 & AK-74M with support, and then add the support AR DPS to see how much it affects things (M4A1 is pretty good on her own!).

Lining up with the DPS table above, we see AK-47 start out with marginally better DPS. Once her skill activates 5 seconds in, she quickly catches up. From ~7 seconds onwards, ignoring one minor dip, AK-47 keeps a decent advantage over G41.

However, the support AR's damage is concerning. We expect G41 & AK-47 to outperform M4/74M as they're getting fairly substantial tiles, but AK-74M is really far down. She has her own permanent 5% FP & ROF buff, but that's a far cry from M4A1's 75% selfbuff (and crazy mod3 SPEQ).

Now, the same chart but adding G41 & M4's numbers as well as 47 & 74M's:

AK-74M's DPS drags down the team, and G41+M4A1 have better performance no matter how long the battle goes on for.

Conclusion#

It looks like AK-47 can deal a surprising amount of damage when you have everything set up right. However, if your third AR isn't getting amazing value out of AK-74M, it seems to still be a net negative for echelon DPS compared to standard options. And even those "standard options" pale in comparison to the very top meta ARs (e.g., G11/416 mods), though those are a bit harder to plot on a graph like this.

This only sort of works at day, and AK-47's tiles aren't helpful (not that those are a major consideration). It ultimately looks better than SUB-2000-based echelons, at least - those waste an entire echelon's worth of data for absolutely no performance return.

Notes#

  • Field testing, as always, will be helpful once these toys actually arrive on EN. Theoretical calculations only get you so far.
  • As you may have noticed from the table earlier, AK-47's SPEQ is a necessary part to keep her somewhat competitive. This will not work at night.
  • While concerns over low accuracy are often unfounded, AK-47's accuracy is actually bad - 37 at lv100, with 100 affection. Her out-of-skill DPS will suffer more than her competitors if your enemies have somewhat more evasion than typical, if you're not running dolls/fairies that give accuracy.
Now, even setting aside 74M's contribution, she struggles to keep up with G41 in <15s fights.
