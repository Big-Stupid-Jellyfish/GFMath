    Protocal Assimilation Resource Statistics

    By BigStupidJellyfish | Last updated 2021-August-26

    Disclaimer: numbers presented here are the result of my own data records. They are only an estimation of the actual rates, and individual luck will vary.

    Coalition Unit size distribution#

    SF unit sizes are not uniformly distributed, and instead follow a bell-shaped curve. Below are the frequencies of each size from my pulls:

    Shiny rate#

    I do not currently have enough data for a good estimate on the shiny rate. As of last update, I have 3 shinies from 259 total pulls (1.158% rate). Due to the very low expected rate, quite a bit more data is needed to have any level of confidence on the true rate.

    Coalition Drill: Decoding Practice rates#

    The chances of getting Basic/Advanced Training Keycodes are also not even. My personal results are shown above, which indicate a 25% advanced rate is very likely.

    Keep in mind - as there is no good reason to believe these rates vary by user or time, given enough simulations, the odds of having a different overall advanced keycode rate approach zero. For example, assuming a true 25% advanced rate over 100 attempts (~2 months), less than 10% of people will have an advanced rate below 20%. After 500 attempts, less than 0.4% of people would.

    SF skills take around 1.75:1 Basic:Advanced keycodes to train, so if you fully level all skills you'll build up a surplus of basic keycodes over time (as they're handed out at a 3:1 ratio). The most efficient svarog bundle only including 1000 basic keycodes doesn't help either.

    (My understanding is that 25% is the commonly accepted rate on foreign servers, although I'm not certain on its basis. If the odds have been datamined, please do let me know and I can simplify this section.)
