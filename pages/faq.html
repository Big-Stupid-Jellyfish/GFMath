    Frequently Asked Questions

    None, so far. Here's a few I made up though:

    • I have a specific question about a post on here

    If you want to see some discussion on each post, there are usually a few comments on my submissions to Reddit. Not being cute fanart means the traffic is pretty limited. Feel free to contact me if you have any further questions. I am also (sometimes) reachable as BigStupidJellyfish#6974 on Discord.

    • X new doll/fairy/equipment/thing isn't mentioned, is it bad?

    Due to the nature of a live-service game, there is new content constantly being released which will change what strategies/echelons are best. Make sure to check the date of these articles and keep that in mind when reading. I try to update pages when I see a major need, but I can't be perfect.

    • How do you make your plots?

    Almost all my plots/calculations are done in Matlab or R. This website is hosted using Github pages and written in raw html because I don't know how to make websites.
