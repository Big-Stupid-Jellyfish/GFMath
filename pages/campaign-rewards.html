    Permanent Campaign Rewards

    By BigStupidJellyfish | 2020-December-27

    As the permanent campaign list is getting pretty lengthy, some of us may not have cleared all the missions. To help in figuring out which missions are worth it, I've compiled tables of all mission rewards in these campaigns.

    Data, calibration tickets, and memory fragments are also assigned approximate gem values, totaled up for each campaign. Values use the 20-gem daily energy pack (0.15 energy/gem). Resources and production tickets are purchasable with gems, but they're easy to get through other means and expensive enough that they aren't considered in the total value.

    If you're looking to farm any particular doll here, I recommend Ceia's channel, which contains farming guides for every event.

    Notable Farmable Dolls/Equipment

    As Cube is entirely at night, the dolls in it are a massive pain to farm. Luckily, the permanent campaign doesn't have anything particularly necessary. Z-63 is a decent 3* molotov and 6P62 is funny as she's an AR that can equip AP rounds (she underperforms compared to almost every alternative though).

    All unique drops: (3*) Z-62, 6P62, 62 Shiki, ASh-12.7, ARX-160, (4*) Gr PSG-1.

    Notable Farmable Dolls/Equipment

    HK45 (E1-2) is a decent frontline FP-buffer for nodupe players. The main point of interest here is the UMP sister SPEQ. It's not significantly stronger than a gold T-exo as it provides +35 evasion at most, but it is a free gold T-exo which can be hard to come by. UMP45 and UMP9 are excellent dolls in their own right, so you'd be able to get some use out of it. I recommend farming it on E3-2, though it can be gotten as early as E2-3.

    All unique drops: (3*) AR70, SSG 69, F1, Gr HK45, EVO 3, (4*) PzB39, (equip) UMP UX Exoskeleton.

    Notable Farmable Dolls/Equipment

    HK416's SPEQ is farmable in Singularity. It's a fairly low priority as its benefits over a gold crit scope are debatable, but if you want it, I'd check out Promotion IV and farm the ambush nodes. MP7 is a good maintank for longer/boss battles, best farmed in Re-Ignition IV (Chapter 1). Serdyukov is a decent nodupe FP buffer but the rest of the 3* dolls are forgettable. You can get Thunder from Stroke Dead-Center II (Chapter 1), but she has few practical uses.

    All unique drops: (3*) Serdyukov, M12, wz.29, TMP, P226, Gepard M1, (4*) Thunder, (5*) MP7, (equipment) EOT XPS3 for 416.

    Notable Farmable Dolls/Equipment

    Python is far and away the best farmable doll in a campaign so far. She generally acts as a strong FP buffer who can deal some damage herself and pass around more buffs on the side. You can use her to even greater effect in teams with AS Val at night, in RFHG teams with Carcano M1891, or in 5HG teams now even stronger with the release of Desert Eagle. The low-requirement farm is Neural Fragments in Chapter 1, but if you can make it to the end Stumbling Block in Chapter 3 has a highly efficient route I like.

    Other than that, T65 is a decent night AR and is available on some maps throughout. M870 is a good 5* shotgun farmable on Darkest Desire (Chapter 1). While not extraordinary, she's a lot cheaper to get than any production SG. Mk 12 and A91 are farmable here, but they are very optional.

    All unique drops: (3*) T65, Type56R, OTs-39, MP-443, Gr MG23, (4*) Mk 12, A91, (5*) M870, Python.
