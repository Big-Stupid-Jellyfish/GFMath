Night Equipment: M4A1

By BigStupidJellyfish | 2022-May-13
  • M4A1's normal attacks can miss and can crit.
  • M4A1's cannon attacks cannot miss and cannot crit on the main target. It does not ignore armor.
  • Her splash damage can miss and can crit.

So, should she use a PEQ at night? Let's make a table.

Scope Normal shots Cannon shots Cannon splash
PEQ Deals damage normally Deals damage normally Deals damage normally
VFL Deals minimal damage due to misses Deals damage normally Deals minimal damage due to misses
EOT Deals minimal damage due to misses Deals damage normally (+some FP, -some ROF) Deals minimal damage due to misses

PEQs are equal or better to the other options across the board. Use them. I guess VFLs are technically usable if exclusively shooting stuff with 0 EVA, but that's not very likely.

If available, her mod SPEQ is preferred. Even if not taking advantage of its armor, it provides a similar DPS boost to her cannon shots as a chip would and has a significant advantage in allowing her normal shots/splash to deal more crit damage.
