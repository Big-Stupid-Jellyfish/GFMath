Limited Event Dolls

By BigStupidJellyfish | Last updated 2023-October-17

Dolls originally introduced as clear rewards for major events or as point/bingo rewards are not permanently available. Instead, they join a pool of similar dolls from which a selection is made farmable with each major/minor/collab event (this page gives a good summary of how this works).

For dolls that were introduced as farmable drops, login dolls, and more, see my page on the permanent campaign rewards.

As time goes on, the number of these has grown by quite a bit. Here's a complete list of such event-limited dolls, and their last known time available. Unless otherwise noted, dolls in this list are not currently or permanently available. They will all be rerun in time, even if some are forgotten for extended periods.

For doll runs after this page's last update, this document may be useful.

Some dolls are made available for farming in an event but are also available elsewhere - the Far Side of the Sea summer event, for example, once had M82A1/Python/Mk 12/M870 as its drops. Everything but M82A1 there was also permanently available in the Continuum Turbulence campaign. Those dolls will be listed in the "currently farmable" table but not the master list of limited dolls. Desert Eagle is also available for new players through an introductory login event.
