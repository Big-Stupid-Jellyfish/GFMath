By BigStupidJellyfish | 2022-May-11 | Updated 2022-June-29

What is this?#

Adding enemies to target saving with GFAlarm is easy - just go to GFLMaps, find their Team ID, and enter it into the Custom Target Train field. However, scrolling through all the maps to find the enemies you're looking for can be a pain. Following are collections of enemy lists based around some theme/utility.

This page is largely for my own use, as few players probably use GFAlarm or even target saving heavily. I'll add more sets over time.
